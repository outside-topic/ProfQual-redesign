Information for Approved Centre

  2. Info For Approved Center

Learner Management

  • After becoming an Approved or Accredited Institute, it will receive access information and guideline for Portal Management
  • Need to register learner in against of every course through portal
  • After registration, cenre will receive invoice & need to pay
  • Once payment done, centre will receive registration confirmation for each learner
  • All learners’ activity [assignment/script] need to be submitted through portal
  • Once qualify, every centre will receive certificate for each learner [electronic/printed copy]

Quality Management

  • After becoming an approved or accredited centre, every centre must comply with ProfQual Quality the Assurance Procedure
  • Centre will receive quality guidelines to follow
  • Centre will receive Training or adequate support to maintain quality at every stage
  • Centre will get prompt support for managing quality
  • Centre will ensure the quality of the learner at the time of intake or registration
  • Centre will ensure the quality of teacher/instructor/trainer
  • Centre will ensure the quality of learning instruments/ tools/ reference study material
  • Centre will ensure the quality of assessment tool

Contract Management

  • Every centre will receive approved stats or accreditation status for a specific period of time
  • Every centre should apply for renewal at least two months prior to the expiry date
  • Every centre must be pay the renewal fee within 10 days of generating the invoice
  • Centre may be required to submit any document or record at the time of evaluation for the renewal of the contract
  • Centre must follow strict guideline for sing logo/reference while promoting the program or issuing certificate
