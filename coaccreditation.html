Features of ProfQual Accredited Courses

ProfQual provides Accreditation support towards education institute, training centre, Business School for their own courses

ProfQual accreditation depends on Industry specific curriculum, application based learning tools, Competency based assessment tools.

Due to UK-standard qualification, Competency based Assessment, ProfQual accredited courses are acceptable worldwide.

ProfQual accredited courses are trusted by millions of professionals from different discipline across the globe.

ProfQual assures UK-standard qualification with very affordable fees based on the motto: Standard Qualification for all

All Accredited Centre or Institute by ProfQual can increase their visibility in their own arena by providing ProfQual accredited Course

All Accredited institutes can use the accreditation logo for the program in promotional material and certificates
