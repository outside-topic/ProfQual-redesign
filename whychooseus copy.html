Trust & Credibility

Awarding Body You Can Rely On

ProfQual operates under the direct regulation of Ofqual, the UK's qualifications regulator. This means every qualification we award carries the full weight and credibility of the UK regulated qualifications framework.

  • Registered with Ofqual as a recognised awarding organisation
  • All qualifications listed on the Regulated Qualifications Framework (RQF)
  • Regular external quality assurance audits and reviews
  • Transparent fee structure with no hidden costs
  • Active member of key industry and education bodies
  • Secure, verifiable digital certification system
