Bespoke Qualifications: Develop your own qualifications

ProfQual has established a broad variety of targeted controlled credentials specifically for centers approved by ProfQual that have funded their growth and partnered with ProfQual to ensure that the curriculum in specialty areas addresses their learner needs. This is ideal if you’re in a highly specialized business, or you’re operating in a unique market where our existing ‘off-the-shelf’ qualifications don’t quite meet your requirements.

ProfQual also open to support Institutes or organization to develop their own qualification with the support of ProfQual expert team.

Procedure to develop bespoke qualification

  • Centre will apply for any bespoke qualification
  • Centre will provide required information to the expert team of ProfQual
  • ProfQual will issue an invoice
  • Centre will pay the fees
  • ProfQual expert team will start working closely with Centre representative
  • Get approval from the Accreditation Council of ProfQual
